About Us

Your Trusted Sri Lanka Tour Partner

Sun Down Tours Sri Lanka is a trusted local travel company specializing in private Sri Lanka tours, tailor-made holiday packages, and chauffeur driven round tours across the island. We are passionate about showcasing the true beauty of Sri Lanka, from golden beaches and ancient cultural sites to misty mountains and wildlife safaris.

With years of experience in the tourism industry, we provide personalized travel experiences designed to match your budget, interests, and travel style. Whether you are planning a luxury private tour, a family holiday, honeymoon trip, or a one day excursion, our expert team ensures a comfortable, safe, and unforgettable journey in Sri Lanka.

We pride ourselves on offering reliable chauffeur guides, modern air-conditioned vehicles, and carefully planned itineraries covering top destinations like Sigiriya, Kandy, Ella, Nuwara Eliya, Galle, and Yala National Park. Our goal is to provide authentic Sri Lanka travel experiences with excellent service and great value for money.
